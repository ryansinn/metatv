## How resolution flows (no manual triage)

1. A flagged item is picked up → becomes a PR with real `test_steps` (the
   testing set) **and** `addresses=("flagged:<uuid>")` on its What's New entry.
2. On the next build the checklist files that flag under **✓ Resolved** with an
   *Addressed PR #N* badge; it leaves the active Flagged list automatically.
3. The tester re-tests via the entry's `test_steps`; when satisfied, removes the
   resolved card. No "go back through flagged items and figure out if they were
   resolved" step.
